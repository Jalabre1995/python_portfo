03 : About me

Just guy that wants to spread his love for coding, AI, Python, and building toy robots to everyone.

I didn't get into coding until I graduated from college in 2018. I took the normal route. Go to college and major in Business. I thought that was my wa out. But I didn't know what I want to do with my business degree. Then a year ago, as I was playing Final Fantasy, I noticed my friend coding for a hackerthon competiton. I was very interested in how he was manipulating the data to make it his own. I looked confused, but at the same time curious.

Before Coding

Just another lost puppy that wanted to find meaning to life. Not sure where my life was taking me. My love for robots, AI, and coding was a tiny flame that didn't ignite at all through high school and college. I wanted to fit in and be like everyone else. I wanted to be an accountant. And don't get me wrong, I am not bashing any accountants out there. You guys are strong building blcoks to this community, but I didn't like what I was doing. I was bored. A mindless zombie you may add. When I thought back to the time I saw my frind codding, thats when the fire inside me came back to life. I dropped being an accountant, and focus so much of my time building toy robots and coding.

Hobbies and Goals

What I love to do outside of coding is build robotic cars and toy figures. I also play guitar and train for half marathons.
